Kamameshi

Description

Ingredients

  • 750g rice
  • 1.2 litres water
  • 2 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 150g diced chicken thigh
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 150g green beans, cut into 2cm lengths
  • 100g chestnuts in syrup, chopped
  • 25g walnuts for topping
  • 5 tbsp concentrated tsuyu soup base
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 tbsp cooking sake
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice: Was the rice and allow to soak in 1.2 litres of water for 30 min.
  2. Add 2 dried shiitake mushrooms to the rice: allow to soak for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add the sugar: After 10 minutes, remove the shiitake mushrooms and add the sugar to the rice and allow to rest for a further 30 minutes. Cover the rice with cling film.
  4. Cut the vegetables: You can leave the skin on the carrots if you want. If you have a food cutter available, cutting the carrot slices into shapes adds a nice decorative touch to the final product.
  5. Add the rice and water to the rice cooker: add 5 tbsp tsuyu soup base, 1 tsp salt, 5 tbsp cooking sake, and 1 tbsp olive oil. Add 150g of diced chicken thigh, carrot clices, and shiitake mushroom. Then switch on the rice cooker.
  6. Prepare the green beans: While the rice is cooking, blanch 150g green beans in boiling water for a few minutes. Then run under cold water, and set aside.
  7. Serve and enjoy: After the rice has finished, stir the rice until everything is nicely dispersed. Top with the blanched green beans, 100g chopped chestnuts in syrup, and 25g walnuts.
